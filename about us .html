About Me

I'm Tiffy (Tiffany) a born and “bread” 20-something South African gal. I'm a food lover, self-taught chef, baker, food photographer & videographer. I started my blog as a hobby in lockdown 1.0, but it has since become my full-time job and biggest passion! My main inspiration for sharing easy and healthy recipes comes from my past of disordered, restrictive eating habits and my struggles with a hormonal disorder – PCOS. I'm at my happiest when helping & serving others and I hope that my recipes can help YOU make healthier choices, without the “FAD” diets and restriction, but instead by making recipes using healthier ingredients. I like to think of my recipes as “good for your soul and your body”. OH, and my recipes are minimal-effort, so that even the busiest people can make them!
